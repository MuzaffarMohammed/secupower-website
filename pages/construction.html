Precasting

Precast concrete is a form of product produced by casting concrete in a reusable mold which is prepared, cast and cured, generally in a curbed manufactory environment, using recyclable moulds. Precast concrete components can be attach to one another to form a absolute structure. It is consistently used for structural components such as; columns, beams, floors, wall panels, staircases, pipes, tunnels, etc.
