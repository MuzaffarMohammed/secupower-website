About Us

SecuPower Contracting Company is a fully Saudi owned organization under commercial registration number 1010335813 with the ministry of Commerce & Industry. The company has its Head office in Riyadh, with its address as P.O. Box-28062, Riyadh-11437.


The Company’s initial activity was subcontracting of Electrical Installations In 2015, the Company entered in to a JV (Joint Venture) with Overseas Constrution Company,(Pvt) Ltd to expand its activities to become a broad-line Contracting Comapny.Today, SecuPower is a full fledged Contracting Engineering and Services and organization in Saudi Arabia.
